Help/Tips

[Hidden Features]

★ Right click on any dress to go to the store to purchase that dress or something similar.
★ You can use you keyboard to slide through images.
--- ┖Use the ('+' keyboard button) to slide to the next image
--- ┖Use the ('-' keyboard button) to slide to the previous Image
★NOTE: On some keyboards you may have to use the (SHIFT+ '-') keyboard button or the (SHIFT+ '+') keyboard button
